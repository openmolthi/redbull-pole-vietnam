The Vision
From Saigon to the World Stage

A 5-year arc from grassroots progression sessions to global competition — with Vietnam as the spiritual home.

Year 1 — 2026
Red Bull Pole Sessions: Vietnam
Non-competitive progression events in Saigon. Community-building. Content creation. Talent scouting. Following the Formation model — start with empowerment, not competition.
Year 2 — 2027
National Competition Circuit
Qualifiers in HCMC, Hanoi, Da Nang. First "Red Bull Pole [Name]" branded competition. 5-10 country qualifiers across Southeast Asia.
Year 3 — 2028
Southeast Asian Expansion
Regional finals. 20+ country qualifiers. Red Bull athlete sponsorships for top Vietnamese pole dancers. Documentary series.
Year 4 — 2029
Global World Final
World Final hosted in Vietnam. 40+ countries. Red Bull Media House full production. Vietnam established as the sport's spiritual home.
Year 5 — 2030
Olympic Push
Pole sport legitimized at global level. GAISF full membership. Olympic consideration. Vietnam's name on the global sports map — permanently.
